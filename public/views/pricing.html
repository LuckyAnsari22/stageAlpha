📐 Lerner Index Pricing Formula

P* = MC × ( |ε| / (|ε| - 1) ) × Season_Multiplier

Where P* = optimal price, MC = marginal cost (40% of base price), ε = price elasticity of demand (estimated via OLS regression on historical booking data), and Season_Multiplier = 1.25 for peak (Nov-Feb), 1.0 for regular, 0.85 for off-season (Jun-Sep). The elasticity is computed using PostgreSQL's built-in REGR_SLOPE aggregate function.
